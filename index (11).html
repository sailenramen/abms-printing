About

Engineering Expertise Meets Cutting-Edge Fabrication

As a lecturer in mechanical engineering at Swinburne University of Technology, I bring academic rigour and real-world precision to every print job. My deep understanding of material science and design tolerances ensures your project is optimised from concept to final part.

Whether you're a student building a university project, an engineer needing functional prototypes, or a business requiring small-batch production, I provide professional-grade 3D printing services tailored to your exact specifications.

What We Offer

Professional Services

From rapid prototyping to full production runs — engineered for precision, delivered on time.

🖨️

Custom 3D Printing

No 3D file? No problem — just send a photo or sketch and we'll model it for you, then print it to spec.

Learn More →
We support all standard file formats and can advise on design optimisation for FDM printing. Layer heights from 0.1mm to 0.3mm available with full colour and material selection.
⚙️

Engineering Prototypes

Functional, dimensionally accurate prototypes for mechanical testing and design validation.

Learn More →
Tolerances to ±0.2mm. Full post-processing available including sanding, priming, and painting. Engineering review included for structural prints.
💡

Product Design & Modelling

CAD design services from concept to print-ready file, with engineering consultation.

Learn More →
Full CAD modelling using Fusion 360 and SolidWorks. Design for manufacturing (DFM) review ensures your model prints correctly first time.
🏭

Small Batch Production

Cost-effective short runs of 5–100 identical parts with consistent quality across the batch.

Learn More →
Batch pricing available from 5+ units. Quality control included. Ideal for startups, product testing, and market validation runs.
🔩

Post-Processing & Finishing

Sanding, painting, assembly, and surface treatment for professional-quality final parts.

Learn More →
Acetone vapour smoothing for ABS. Epoxy coating for watertight parts. Spray painting and primer available. Threaded insert installation included.

How It Works

The Print Process

Simple, transparent, and professional from start to delivery.

📁

Submit Design

No 3D file? No problem. Just send us a photo, sketch, or reference image of what you want — we handle the modelling from there.

💬

Review & Quote

We review your design within 2 hours, check for printability issues, and send you a detailed quote with options.

🖨️

Precision Printing

Once approved, we print your part on the Bambu Lab P1S with continuous quality monitoring throughout the print.

📦

Delivery

Pickup from Tarneit or express shipping anywhere in Australia. Parts are packaged to prevent any damage in transit.
